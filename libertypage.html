The Liberty 1&2 Theater

The historic Liberty 1 & 2 Theaters is located in beautiful downtown Libertyville. We at Golden Age Cinemas strive to provide affordable entertainment for the whole family in a fun, clean, and friendly environment. The Liberty 1 & 2 Theater was once the employer of a young Marlon Brando. It is our goal to try to recreate that era of golden age of movie-going within our walls.

FAQs

What time does the theater open?

We open 30 minutes before the first showtime of the day.

What movies will be playing on xx/xx/xx?

Movie times will be posted on the Tuesday prior to the upcoming week

Do you offer discounts for military?

Yes! Tickets are always $6 and concessions are buy one get one free.

Can I host an event at the theater?

Yes. Please email Liberty12Events@gmail.com with your contact info and we will get back to you asap.
